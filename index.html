1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Data

We request access to your device's location (GPS and network-based) for the sole purpose of calculating accurate Prayer Times and determining the Qibla direction relative to your position.

  • Precise Location: Used to provide the most accurate prayer times for your specific coordinates.
  • Approximate Location: Used to identify your city/region for prayer time adjustments.

This location data is processed locally on your device or sent ephemerally to our prayer time calculation API. We do not store your location history on our servers.

1.2 Device Information

We may collect non-personal device information such as device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers to ensure app compatibility and improve performance.
